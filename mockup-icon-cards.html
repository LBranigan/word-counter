Context & Design Notes

Design Features:

  • Horizontal flex layout with 4 compact cards
  • Subtle backgrounds (#fafbfc) with light borders (#e1e4e8)
  • Minimal visual weight - feels like gentle hints
  • Each card has a colored top border for subtle differentiation
  • Hover effects for interactivity (lift + shadow)
  • Icons/emojis paired with brief text (1-2 lines)
  • Responsive - wraps nicely on tablets, stacks on mobile
  • Condensed from 5 bullet points to 4 essential tips
  • Muted color palette - soft blues, greens, golds, purples
  • Professional and polished appearance
